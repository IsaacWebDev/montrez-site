# Luxury Intro Animation - Client Summary

## What We Built

A **10-second premium entrance animation** for your Montrez website that creates an unforgettable first impression. Think of it like entering an exclusive chateau estate.

## The Experience

When visitors arrive at your site, they'll experience:

### 🚪 **Phase 1: The Gates (0-3 seconds)**
Iron gates swing open, revealing the path ahead. Your brand name appears elegantly in the center.

### 🌊 **Phase 2: The Approach (3-6 seconds)**
Camera glides through the gates, a serene lake comes into view. The journey toward the chateau begins.

### 🏰 **Phase 3: The Entrance (6-9 seconds)**
Grand chateau doors open with a dramatic 3D effect, inviting visitors inside.

### ✨ **Phase 4: Welcome (9-10 seconds)**
Smooth fade to white, then your website appears—ready for them to explore.
