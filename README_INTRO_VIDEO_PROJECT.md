## 🚨 Common Questions

### Q: Can I create this video myself?
**A:** Technically yes, but not recommended. You'd need:
- 12-18 hours of your time
- After Effects or Blender skills
- Stock footage budget ($30-80)
- Result quality: 6/10 vs. pro 9/10

**Verdict:** Your time is worth more. Hire a pro for $125.

---

### Q: What if the animator delivers a file >500KB?
**A:** Request re-compression. This is a hard requirement.
- Target bitrate: 800-1000 kbps
- Remove audio track (saves 30-40%)
- Use two-pass encoding
- Acceptable max: 650KB (if quality demands it)

Templates include exact language to request this fix.

---

### Q: What if the video won't autoplay on iOS?
**A:** This is already handled in the code. Ensure:
```jsx
<video muted playsInline autoPlay>
```
- `muted` = REQUIRED for autoplay
- `playsInline` = REQUIRED for iOS
- `autoPlay` = Triggers autoplay

If still fails, troubleshooting guide covers this.

---

### Q: Fiverr or Upwork - which is better?
**A:** Both work, slight differences:

**Fiverr:**
- ✅ Easier to browse portfolios
- ✅ Fixed pricing (less negotiation)
- ✅ Faster hiring process
- ❌ Lower average quality

**Upwork:**
- ✅ Higher quality professionals
- ✅ Better for complex projects
- ✅ Milestone-based payments
- ❌ More time to hire (proposal review)

**Recommendation:** Post on BOTH, hire best candidate.

---

### Q: What if no one can hit <500KB?
**A:** Options:
1. **Accept 650KB** (still fast, higher quality)
2. **Request more compression** (lower quality)
3. **Create 2 versions:**
   - Desktop: 650KB (high quality)
   - Mobile: 350KB (compressed)

650KB is acceptable if quality justifies it.
