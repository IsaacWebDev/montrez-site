## 🚨 Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue: Video file >500KB
**Solution:** Ask animator to re-compress
- Target bitrate: 800-1000 kbps
- Remove audio track (saves ~30-40%)
- Two-pass encoding

### Issue: Video won't autoplay on iPhone
**Solution:** Verify these attributes exist
```jsx
<video muted playsInline autoPlay>
```
- `muted` = REQUIRED for autoplay
- `playsInline` = REQUIRED for iOS
- `autoPlay` = Triggers autoplay

### Issue: Quality looks bad (blocky/blurry)
**Solution:** Request higher quality version
- Acceptable file size: up to 650KB
- Higher bitrate: 1200-1500 kbps
- Balance quality vs. load time

### Issue: Animator delivers wrong format
**Solution:** Request correct specs
- Format: MP4 (not AVI, MOV, WebM)
- Codec: H.264 (not H.265, VP9)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (not 4K, not vertical)
