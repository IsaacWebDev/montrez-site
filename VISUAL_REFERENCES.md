## рҹҺЁ COLOR USAGE EXAMPLES

### Electric Blue (#00F0FF) Placement

```
вң… USE electric blue for:
- Primary CTA buttons (background)
- Hover states (glow, border)
- Active states (navigation, filters)
- Price tags
- "SOLD OUT" badges
- Promo codes
- Underlines on headings
- Icons (cart count, accent icons)
- Loading spinners
- Progress bars

вқҢ DON'T use electric blue for:
- Body text (readability)
- Large backgrounds (overwhelming)
- More than 5% of total color palette
- Borders on every element (dilutes impact)
```

### Example Component Breakdown

**Homepage Hero:**
- Background: Black (#000000) - 85%
- Text: White (#FFFFFF) - 10%
- CTA Button: Electric Blue (#00F0FF) - 5%

**Product Card:**
- Card BG: Near-black (#1A1A1A) - 90%
- Text: White (#FFFFFF) - 8%
- Price + Hover Glow: Electric Blue (#00F0FF) - 2%

**Navigation Menu:**
- Menu BG: Black (#000000) - 95%
- Text: White (#FFFFFF) - 4%
- Active Accent Bar: Electric Blue (#00F0FF) - 1%

**Ratio:** 80-85% Black/White, 10-15% Gray, 5% Electric Blue (MAX)

## рҹ–ҘпёҸ DESKTOP vs MOBILE LAYOUT

### Desktop (>1024px)
- Asymmetric product grids (2fr 1fr 1fr)
- Side-by-side product images + details
- Hover states active (glow effects)
- Full navigation visible

### Tablet (768px-1024px)
- 2-column product grids
- Stacked product detail layout
- Touch-friendly buttons (48x48px)
- Hamburger navigation

### Mobile (<768px)
- Single column layouts
- Full-width CTAs
- Larger touch targets (56x56px)
- Bottom sticky CTA bar
- Minimal text, maximum impact
