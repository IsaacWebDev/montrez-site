**Status:** 🔴 Blocking purchases

**Impact:** $X revenue potential

- No item display

## 3. NO MOBILE TOUCH OPTIMIZATION
**Status:** 🔴 Affects 80% of users  
**Fix Time:** 2-3 hours  
**Impact:** +30-50% mobile conversion

- Navbar icons too small
- Filter buttons hard to tap
- Size selectors cramped
- No focus indicators

This alone causes frustration, drops, bounces.

- Stock warnings (0)
- Recently sold counts (0)
- Security badges (0)

Competitors have all of these. Users trust them more.
