## Alternate Minimal Version

If keeping it ultra-minimal (like brand aesthetic):

**Headline**: "Contact"

**Body**: "For inquiries: contact@montrezofficial.com"

**Social**: Instagram icon → @montrezofficial

**That's it.** (Matches mysterious/exclusive vibe)

---

## Recommendation

Use the full form version but keep design minimal and sleek. The form gives professionalism while email-only might seem too gatekeep-y (though that could work for ultra-exclusive feel).

**Best approach**: Form + direct email option + response time = accessible but still premium.
