## Quick Overview
I'm looking for a talented motion designer/animator to create a luxury brand intro video for MONTREZ, a high-end streetwear brand. The animation features an ornate iron gate opening, camera zoom through a scenic lake, and dramatic chateau doors opening before fading to the website.

**Style:** High-end fashion (think Dior, Chanel, Louis Vuitton)  
**Duration:** 3-5 seconds  
**Budget:** $100-150 USD  
**Timeline:** 3-5 business days

## What I Need

### Animation Sequence
1. **Scene 1 (0-1s):** Camera facing ornate iron gates → gates slowly swing open
2. **Scene 2 (1-2.5s):** Camera zooms forward, revealing elegant lake between gates and chateau
3. **Scene 3 (2.5-4s):** Camera crosses lake, approaching chateau
4. **Scene 4 (4-5s):** Chateau doors dramatically open (majestic, welcoming)
5. **Scene 5 (5s):** Smooth fade to white → website loads

### Visual Style
- **Aesthetic:** Luxury fashion brand (NOT fast fashion or tech startup)
- **Colors:** Deep blacks, rich golds, cream/ivory tones
- **Lighting:** Golden hour, soft diffused lighting
- **Mood:** Elegant, mysterious, exclusive, aspirational
- **References:** Dior brand films, Versailles architecture, high-fashion commercials

### Technical Requirements
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD minimum)
- **Frame Rate:** 60fps (smooth cinematic motion)
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **File Size:** MUST be <500KB (critical for web performance)
  - Use aggressive compression while maintaining quality
  - Remove audio track if not essential
  - Two-pass encoding recommended
- **Audio:** Optional subtle luxury music (OR no audio preferred for web autoplay)
